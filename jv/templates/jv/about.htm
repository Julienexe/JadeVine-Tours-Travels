About Us

Jadevine Travel & Tours company was established with a greater good purpose towards it's esteemed customers, and Year after Year has been expanding its business throughout the tourism sector in Zanzibar-Tanzania.
 
We work in partnership with both large and small tour operators around the World in order to create portforms of accommodation units that satisfy the needs of each market segment. The knowledge and accumulated experience of both local and international markets made the company the largest incoming Tour agency in Zanzibar  and a highly reputable, professional and organized travel & tours enterprise.
 
We organize Safari tours(Main-land tours) and Excursions in small and large groups & other services include Airport representations.etc which are available to all holidaymakers in Zanzibar-Tanzania.

 

OUR TEAM:

Our team is one of the most valuable resources.  Our experience, knowledge and commitment constitute a major factor in the company’s success.
 
Our culture is self orientated towards customer satisfaction as each of us partakes pride in creating lasting memories for holiday tourists at the island of Zanzibar, while putting their safety as one of our top priorities.
 
Training is a regular business activity amongst workers & Management, particularly in technological advancements and communication systems, in order to ensure the continuity in communication, accounting and book keeping systems between us and our clients.

 

 MISSION, GOALS & VISION

 

Mission:
To ensure satisfaction and safety of our customers.
To ensure that we do not merely just show new places to our fellow customers but actually extend our knowledge of different ethnic cultures to our visitors in Zanzibar-Tanzania.

 

Goals:
To develop consistent and long-term relationships with our clients and suppliers also making them happy and satisfied is paramount.


 Vision:
