Our Services

HOTELS, LODGES & VILLAS

Book for your accommodation with us. There's a wealth of Hotels, Lodges and Villas in Zanzibar with fine hospitality to suit a range of budgets. Most Hotels occupy historical or restored buildings with a short walking distance of the main attractions, And all have the mod cons. Many have indoors/outdoor pools and an Ocean view. Most Hotels are dotted along the archipelago's coastline especially in the north and east side of the island of Zanzibar. Many are part of the luxury beach Hotels, Lodges and villas offering everything any one need to have such as a Serene and indulgent environment to stay.
